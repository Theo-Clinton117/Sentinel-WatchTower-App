Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Sentinel WatchTower is built to help people stay informed and safe. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide when creating an account, submitting alerts, or requesting support. This may include name, email, phone number, and alert details. If you enable location features, we process your location to power risk intelligence, alerts, and verification workflows.

How We Use Information

We use your data to operate the platform, verify alerts, notify you of nearby risks, and improve system accuracy. We may aggregate data for analytics and reporting while removing direct identifiers.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with service providers (e.g., payment processors) to deliver the service, or with authorities when required by law.

Data Retention

We retain account and alert data as long as needed to operate WatchTower and meet compliance obligations. You may request deletion by contacting support.

Your Choices

You can update your profile details, opt in/out of notifications, and control location sharing from your device settings. You may request access or deletion of your data via support.

Security

We apply security best practices to protect data in transit and at rest. No system is perfect, but we continuously improve our defenses and monitoring.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Email us at support@sentinelwatchtower.com.
